Department Overview

The Local SEO & GBP Department owns all local search visibility for every client. This includes keeping Google Business Profiles fully optimized and active, maintaining accurate citations across all major directories, and monitoring the local competitive landscape. One specialist handles up to 50 clients using BrightLocal as the central platform for auditing, tracking, and reporting.

GBP Service Deliverables

Six active service areas are included in every GBP management engagement, executed monthly per client.

📝

GBP Posts

Text + AI-generated image

  • Write one GBP post per client per month using AI assistance
  • Post copy tailored to the client's specialty (e.g. "We specialize in root canals — here's what to expect")
  • Generate a matching image using AI (ChatGPT / Midjourney) relevant to the post topic
  • Upload post + image directly to GBP
  • Use client-supplied photos when available; fall back to AI-generated or stock images
  • Review post performance (views, clicks) and adjust topics monthly
❓

Q&A Management

Monitor, seed, and respond

  • Monitor the GBP Q&A section monthly for new questions from the public
  • Respond to unanswered questions within the reporting period
  • Seed 1–2 strategic Q&As per month using common patient/client questions
  • Ensure answers contain relevant local keywords naturally
  • Flag inappropriate or spam questions for removal via Google
⭐

Review Monitoring & Responses

Protect and build reputation

  • Monitor all new reviews via BrightLocal alerts
  • Draft and post responses to all new reviews — positive and negative
  • Escalate negative reviews (3 stars or below) to Account Manager before responding
  • Flag potentially fake or malicious reviews for client to dispute
  • Track review velocity (new reviews per month) and average star rating trend
  • Report on review count and rating changes in monthly report
🔍

Competitor Reporting

Local pack benchmarking

  • Identify 3–5 top local competitors appearing in the Google local pack for client's primary keywords
  • Track competitor review count and rating changes monthly
  • Note competitor GBP activity (new posts, new photos, new services)
  • Flag if a competitor gains a significant review or ranking advantage
  • Include competitive snapshot in monthly report
📸

Photo Uploads

Visual presence maintenance

  • Upload minimum 1 new photo per client per month
  • Source photos from: client-supplied images, AI-generated visuals, or licensed stock
  • Ensure photos are correctly categorized (interior, exterior, team, product, etc.)
  • Add keyword-rich file names and alt descriptions before uploading
  • Monitor for user-uploaded photos that may be inappropriate — flag for removal
  • Track total photo count and monthly views in report
⚙️

Service & Menu Updates

Profile completeness & accuracy

  • Audit GBP profile completeness monthly — target 100% completion score
  • Update services list, descriptions, and pricing when changes are provided by client
  • Ensure business hours, holiday hours, and special hours are always current
  • Verify and update business categories — primary and secondary
  • Maintain accurate website URL, phone number, and address (NAP consistency)
  • Add or update service area pages if the client serves multiple locations

Citation Building & Management

Citations — consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) listings across directories — are a core local ranking factor. BrightLocal is used for auditing, building, and monitoring all citations.

Citation Audit (Onboarding)

  • Run a full citation audit via BrightLocal for every new client
  • Identify existing citations and flag any with incorrect NAP data
  • Identify key directories the client is missing from
  • Prioritize fixes: Google, Yelp, Facebook, Bing Places, Apple Maps first
  • Document all citation sources in a client citation tracker
  • Correct all inaccurate listings within the first 30 days

Ongoing Citation Building

  • Submit client to new high-authority directories monthly (minimum 5 per month)
  • Prioritize industry-specific directories (e.g. Healthgrades, Zocdoc for medical clients)
  • Ensure NAP is 100% consistent across all submissions
  • Track submission status and live citation count in BrightLocal
  • Monitor for duplicate citations and suppress or remove them
  • Report on total citation count growth in monthly report

NAP Consistency Standards

  • Business name must match exactly across all platforms — no abbreviations or variations
  • Phone number format must be consistent (e.g. always (416) 555-0100)
  • Address must match Google Maps exactly, including suite/unit formatting
  • Website URL must be consistent — always use the canonical version with or without www
  • Any business rebrand or address change triggers a full citation update sweep

Priority Directory Targets

Every client should be listed and verified on the following platforms before building secondary directories:

  • Google Business Profile
  • Bing Places for Business
  • Apple Maps Connect
  • Yelp, Facebook, Yellow Pages
  • Industry-specific: Healthgrades, RateMDs, Zocdoc (medical/dental)
  • Canadian: 411.ca, Canada411, LocalPages

Monthly Workflow

Each specialist follows a standardized monthly workflow for every assigned client to ensure consistency and nothing falls through the cracks.

1
GBP Health Check (Week 1)
Log into BrightLocal and review each client's GBP for any Google-suggested edits, ownership issues, or profile suspensions. Confirm profile completeness is at 100%. Flag any issues requiring client input to Account Manager immediately.
2
Review Responses (Week 1)
Check for all new reviews since last month. Draft responses for all reviews — escalate any 3-star or below to the Account Manager before posting. Once approved, publish all responses.
3
GBP Post + Photo (Week 2)
Write the monthly GBP post using AI, focused on a service or seasonal topic relevant to the client. Generate or source a matching image. Upload both to GBP. Upload at least one additional photo to maintain visual freshness.
4
Q&A & Service Update Check (Week 2)
Review the Q&A section for new questions and respond or seed new Q&As. Check if any service updates, hours changes, or special hours need to be applied based on client communications that month.
5
Citation Building (Week 3)
Submit the client to 5 new high-authority or industry-specific directories via BrightLocal. Check existing citations for any new inaccuracies or duplicates and resolve them. Update the citation tracker.
6
Competitor Snapshot (Week 3)
Run a quick local pack check for the client's top 2–3 keywords. Note which competitors appear, their review count and rating, and any notable GBP activity. Document changes from last month.
7
Monthly Report (Week 4)
Pull data from BrightLocal — local rank positions, citation count, review count, GBP insights (views, calls, direction requests). Compile into the standard monthly report template and send to Account Manager for client delivery.
5

Reporting

Monthly Report Contents

  • Local pack rankings for top 5 tracked keywords (vs. prior month)
  • GBP insights: profile views, website clicks, calls, direction requests
  • Review summary: new reviews, average rating, total review count
  • Citation count: total live citations vs. prior month
  • GBP post performance: views and engagement on the month's post
  • Competitor snapshot: top 3 local competitors and their review trends
  • Completed deliverables this month
  • Recommendations and priorities for next month

Key Performance Indicators

These are the metrics the Local SEO team is accountable for moving month over month:

  • Local pack ranking positions for tracked keywords
  • GBP profile views (impressions)
  • Calls and direction requests generated from GBP
  • Review count growth (new reviews per month)
  • Average star rating (target: maintain or improve)
  • Citation count growth (target: +5 minimum per month)
  • GBP profile completeness score (target: 100%)
